About Me

Hi, I’m Shree.

A Games Development student currently in my 3rd year in the UK. I was born and raise in Nepal and moved to the UK in 2022. I originally studied Science in +2 and planned to go into nursing, but when things didn’t go as expected, I took a leap into a completely different world — game development.

I started with almost no background, something completely unfamiliar. Starting from zero was scary, especially when others already had years of experience. but I found myself loving the challenge. Today, I work mainly with C# and Unity, and I’m slowly exploring HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, and Java. I’m not perfect at coding yet, but I love the feeling of being able to build something from nothing — even if it takes time, patience, and a lot of debugging.

Outside of code, I’m happiest listening to music, being in nature, or watching anime. I’m a little nervous, clumsy, and not the best with crowds — but I’m determined. My biggest motivation is giving my parents the life they deserve and one day landing a job at Microsoft.
