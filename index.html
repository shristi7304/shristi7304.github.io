About

Hi, I’m Shree — a 3rd year Games Development student in the UK. I was born and raise in Nepal and moved to the UK in 2022. I originally studied Science in +2 and planned to go into nursing, but when things didn’t go as expected, I took a leap into a completely different world — game development.

I started with almost no background, something completely unfamiliar. Starting from zero was scary, especially when others already had years of experience. but I found myself loving the challenge.

Today, I work mainly with C# & Unity, and I’m exploring Java, LWJGL, HTML/CSS/JS, Python, and SQL.

I’m not perfect, but I love the feeling of being able to build something from nothing — even if it takes time, patience, and a lot of debugging.

Outside of coding: music, nature, and anime 🌸
